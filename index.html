ASSUMPTIONS

Our understanding of the Covid-19 virus is evolving everyday. This simulation is based on preliminary data available in mid-March 2020 and the sources are cited articles below for reference. Please use it as an illustration of how a virus like Covid-19 can spread based on the extent people effectively isolate themselves. The simulation assumes the following:

  • The virus is contagious for 14 days
  • 15% of all cases require hopitalization
  • 3% of all cases result in death
  • There's roughtly 2 available hospital beds per 1000 people in the US
  • People who have gotten the virus and recovered will not infect others
